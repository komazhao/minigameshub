    Last updated:

    Introduction

    Welcome to MiniGamesHub.co ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

    Information We Collect

    • Usage data: pages viewed, clicks, referring/exit pages, time on page, approximate location, device and browser information.
    • Contact data you provide: when you email us or submit a form (e.g., name, email, message).
    • Cookies/local storage: preferences (e.g., favorites), UI settings, and session information.

    Cookies and Similar Technologies

    We use cookies and local storage to remember preferences, improve performance, and understand site usage. You can control cookies in your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect site functionality.

    Advertising (Google AdSense)

    We display ads provided by Google and/or its partners. Third‑party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and/or other websites. Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of some third‑party vendors’ uses of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices.

    For users in the EEA/UK, where required, we will seek consent for the use of cookies and personalized ads. You can change your preferences at any time via your browser or device settings.

    Analytics and Third‑Party Services

    We may use analytics tools to understand usage trends and improve the site. These providers process data as described in their own privacy policies.

    How We Use Information

    • Provide and operate the website and its features (e.g., search, favorites).
    • Improve performance, fix bugs, and enhance user experience.
    • Respond to inquiries and support requests.
    • Measure and support advertising and analytics.

    Children’s Privacy

    We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

    Data Security

    We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, transmission over the internet and electronic storage cannot be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

    Your Choices

    • Manage or block cookies in your browser settings.
    • Opt out of personalized ads via Google Ads Settings and AboutAds.
    • Contact us to update or delete information you have provided via forms or email.

    Contact Us

    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@minigameshub.co.

    Changes to This Policy

    We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the site after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
