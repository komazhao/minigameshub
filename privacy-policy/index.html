    Privacy Policy

    Last updated:

    Introduction

    Welcome to MiniGamesHub.co ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

    Information We Collect

    • Usage data: pages viewed, clicks, referring/exit pages, time on page, approximate location, device and browser information.
    • Contact data you provide: when you email us or submit a form (e.g., name, email, message).
    • Cookies/local storage: preferences (e.g., favorites), UI settings, and session information.

    Cookies and Similar Technologies

    We use cookies and local storage to remember preferences, improve performance, and understand site usage. Third‑party partners may set cookies via embedded scripts. You can control cookies in your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect site functionality.

    Advertising (Google AdSense)

    We display ads via Google AdSense and approved ad technology partners. Third‑party vendors, including Google, use cookies or local storage to deliver, measure, and improve ads, and to limit ad frequency. These may collect IP address, device and browser information, general location, pages viewed, ad interactions, and inferred interests. Learn more at Google Advertising Policies.

    Personalized ads use data to select ads likely to be more relevant to you. If you prefer not to receive personalized ads, you may opt out in Google Ads Settings or use industry tools such as AboutAds. We also provide on‑site controls; see “Consent and Cookie Choices”.

    Data Sharing and Partners

    We may share limited data with analytics and advertising partners to operate and improve our service. This can include IP address, user agent, device type, language, approximate location, pages visited, and ad event data. We do not sell personal information.

    Consent and Cookie Choices (EU/UK)

    Where required (e.g., EEA/UK), we request your consent for analytics and advertising cookies using a Google‑certified Consent Management Platform (CMP) integrated with the IAB TCF v2.2 framework. Until you consent, we show non‑personalized ads and disable analytics. You can manage your choices anytime using the control below:

    Manage Cookie Preferences

    Ad Interaction Policy

    We do not encourage clicking on ads. Users should not be misled, incentivized, or rewarded to interact with ads. Any ad placements are clearly distinguishable from game content.

    Analytics and Third‑Party Services

    We may use analytics tools to understand usage trends and improve the site. These providers process data as described in their own privacy policies.

    How We Use Information

    • Provide and operate the website and its features (e.g., search, favorites).
    • Improve performance, fix bugs, and enhance user experience.
    • Respond to inquiries and support requests.
    • Measure and support advertising and analytics.

    Children’s Privacy

    We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

    Data Security

    We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, transmission over the internet and electronic storage cannot be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

    Your Choices

    • Manage or block cookies in your browser settings.
    • Opt out of personalized ads via Google Ads Settings and AboutAds.
    • Contact us to update or delete information you have provided via forms or email.

    Contact Us

    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@minigameshub.co.

    Changes to This Policy

    We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the site after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
