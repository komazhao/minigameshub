    About MiniGamesHub

    Our mission, team, and content standards.

    Who We Are

    MiniGamesHub.co is a small, passionate team that curates high‑quality HTML5 browser games you can play instantly on any device. We focus on speed, accessibility, and a clean playing experience.

    Our Mission

    Make great mini games easy to discover and safe to play for everyone. We aim to highlight titles that are fun, lightweight, and mobile‑friendly, while keeping the site fast and user‑first.

    Editorial Policy & Content Standards

    • Quality & Playability: We verify basic playability, performance, and responsiveness across devices before featuring a game.
    • Safety: We avoid games that contain illegal content, explicit adult material, hate speech, or promote violence or self‑harm.
    • Attribution: We respect developer copyrights and display publisher information where provided.
    • Age Appropriateness: We strive to keep content suitable for a general audience. Some titles may be better for older players due to difficulty or themes.
    • Removal: If a publisher or rights holder requests removal, we honor legitimate takedown requests promptly. See our Terms for the process.

    If you discover an issue with any game’s content, please contact us at contact@minigameshub.co.

    Advertising & Cookies

    We use privacy‑respecting analytics and may display ads to support operations. Third‑party partners (including Google) may use cookies to deliver and measure ads. For details and your choices, please read our Privacy Policy.

    Contact

    General inquiries: contact@minigameshub.co. You can also reach us via the Contact page.
